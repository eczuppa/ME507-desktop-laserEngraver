Safety Supervising Task. 

This task monitors incoming results from our temperature sensor and limit switches and if an error condition occurs this task sets different flags to make safety stuff happen e.\+g. turning off the laser, turning on the fan before cutting begins, and not letting the cutting start until the top of the laser\textquotesingle{}s enclosure is closed.
