\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
a motor driver class that is specific to Toshiba\textquotesingle{}s \mbox{\hyperlink{classTB6612FNG}{T\+B6612\+F\+NG}} motor driver IC 

makes use of arduino commands to create a simple driver that interfaces with toshiba\textquotesingle{}s \mbox{\hyperlink{classTB6612FNG}{T\+B6612\+F\+NG}} dual H-\/bridge motor driver IC. The P\+WM signal generation is handeled by the Arduino {\ttfamily analog\+Write()} and the direction pin settings for changing the direction of the motor is done with Arduino\textquotesingle{}s {\ttfamily digital\+Write}.
